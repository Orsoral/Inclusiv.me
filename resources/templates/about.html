What is Inclusiv.me ?

Inclusiv.me helps you challenge your behavior to welcome diversity. Your own actions will inspire others to become more inclusive, as you do.

How it works

Inclusiv.me suggests many actions to open up to diversity, leverage powers you didn't know you had and overcome barriers to being more inclusive, specially in those situations where you want to act but don't know how to. On top of practical situations, inclusiv.me also shares resources and self-awareness questions to raise your expectations.

Why be more inclusive ?
